## Research Protocol Per Platform

For each platform, collect:
1. **Identity**: Common name, official designation (MIL-STD nomenclature), manufacturer
2. **Timeline**: Development start, first flight/prototype, IOC, production span
3. **Production**: Units built, current operator quantities, variants produced
4. **Specifications**: All physical dimensions, performance envelope, crew, sensors
5. **Powerplant**: Engine model, type, output, count
6. **Armament**: Primary and secondary weapons, hardpoints, loadout options
7. **Economics**: Unit cost (specify FY dollars), program cost, CPFH
8. **Operators**: US service branches + quantities, foreign military sales
9. **Combat History**: Conflicts, roles, kills/losses, notable engagements
10. **Sources**: Minimum 2 independent URLs per platform
